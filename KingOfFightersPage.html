King Of Fighters

The King of Fighters (KOF)[a] is a series of fighting games by SNK that began with the release of The King of Fighters '94 in 1994. The series was developed originally for SNK's Neo Geo MVS arcade hardware.

The King of Fighters '94 is the first installment of what would later become SNK's longest-running and most successful fighting series.
