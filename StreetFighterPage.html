Street Fighter

Street Fighter, is a Japanese media franchise centered on a series of fighting games developed and published by Capcom. The first game in the series was released in 1987, followed by six other main series games, various spin-offs and crossovers, and numerous appearances in other media.

Its best-selling 1991 release Street Fighter II established many of the conventions of the one-on-one fighting genre.
