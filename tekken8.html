Tekken 8

About Tekken 8

ekken 8 (鉄拳8 lit. Iron Fist 8) is the eighth main installment in the Tekken series. It is a fighting game developed by Bandai Namco Studios and Arika[1] and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was announced on September 13th, 2022, via the PlayStation State of Play.
