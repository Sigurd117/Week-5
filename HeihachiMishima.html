Heihachi Mishima

About Heihachi Mishima

Heihachi Mishima (三島 平八 Mishima Heihachi) is one of the main characters in the Tekken series. He debuted in the first game in the series and has returned for all subsequent titles until Tekken 8. Though seen as the main protagonist of the second game, he is the main antagonist of the series and almost always responsible for the cataclysmic events.
