Tekken 6

About Tekken 6

Tekken 6 (鉄拳6 Tekken Shikkusu, lit. Iron Fist 6) is a fighting game developed and published by Namco. It is the sixth main installment in the Tekken franchise and the first multi-platform title. It was released in Japanese arcades on November 26, 2007 as the first game running on the PlayStation 3-based System 357 arcade board.
